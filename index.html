ONAYTONIE

Contemporary Artist | Photographer

Seattle, WA

As an artist, I create pieces using various media, deeply influenced by quotes, poetry, black culture, and the small details of everyday life. As a phtographer, I strive to freeze little moments in time, preserving the essence of life as it unfolds naturally. Art and photgraphy compels me to look beyond the obvious, to immerse in mental silence, and to discover a deeper mindfulness of creativity.

Creativity is allowing yourself to make mistakes. Art is knowing which ones to keep -Scott Adams
