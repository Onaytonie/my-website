BRAND STORY


Southide Richmond

Growing up in Southside Richmond, Virginia, I wasn't surrounded by art, nor did I take any formal art classes. However, my imagination was always vivid and untamed. As a child, I cherished moments sitting beside my grandmother in church, drawing and coloring to avoid Sunday school. These creative sessions were my sanctuary, rewarded with a piece of her Winterfresh gum and the comfort of being nestled under her arm.

I matured, standing tall at six feet, I found it challenging to discover fashion that resonated with my personality. This led me to hand-paint my clothing, transforming them into unique expressions of self. What began as a personal endeavor soon blossomed into a side hustle, designing custom apparel and tote bags for others on my mom's dining room table.

My artistic journey took a profound turn when I discovered canvas and acrylics; they captivated me and have held my heart ever since. Living in Japan presented an opportunity to bridge cultural and language barriers by teaching paint classes. Through these sessions, I connected with others, proving that art is a universal language.

I am passionate about all forms of art, both digital and physical. Studying the great artists allows me to explore and experiment with various styles, continually evolving my creative expression. In essence, I'm just a girl from Southside who loves to create. My journey is a testament to the power of imagination and the beauty of embracing one's unique path.
